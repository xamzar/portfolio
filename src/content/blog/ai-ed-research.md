Been helping a professor develop a curriculum that integrates AI assistants into a first-year Python course. The question: does AI help or hurt learning?

**Hypothesis:** Guided use of LLMs as a *pair programmer* helps students debug faster and ask better questions, as long as they still write the code themselves.

Early results are promising. Students who use the AI tool spend less time stuck on syntax errors and more time on logic.
