An IPython cell magic that watches your notebook cells, analyzes them through the lens of the Socratic method, and speaks guiding questions aloud via text-to-speech when you go off-track. Stays silent when your code is correct. Triple TTS backend — espeak (0.03s local), edge-tts (cloud neural), kokoro (local neural). Built for university programming students.
